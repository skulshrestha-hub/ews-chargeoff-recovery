Guest Portal
How Members Reach sofi.com/pay
The most common path: member is denied at another bank, receives an adverse action letter naming SoFi/EWS, and needs to pay but can't log in.
1
Adverse Action Letter
Member denied at another bank. Letter names SoFi/EWS. Member calls SoFi, agent directs to sofi.com/pay.
2
Charge-Off Email (Day 57)
"Your account has been closed with a balance of $XX. Pay at sofi.com/pay or log in to the app."
3
Pre-Charge-Off Reminders
Updated emails during 57-day grace period include sofi.com/pay as fallback if account is closed.
4
SoFi Support Agent
Member calls for any charge-off issue. Agent provides URL verbally or via text/email.
5
Help Center / FAQ
"How to pay a charged-off balance and clear your EWS record" article links to portal.
6
In-App "Pay on Web"
Logged-in member can tap "Pay on web instead" to open sofi.com/pay in mobile browser.
