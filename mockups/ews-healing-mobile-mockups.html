About Your Balance
What is Early Warning Services (EWS)?

EWS operates the National Shared Database, used by most U.S. banks and credit unions to screen applicants when they open new deposit accounts.

When an account is closed due to a negative balance (charge-off), this is reported to EWS. Other banks may see this when you try to open a new account.

The good news: You can clear your record by paying off the balance. Once paid, SoFi will update your EWS record within 10 business days.
