Dispute Reported Data
What you can dispute
If you believe the data SoFi reported to Early Warning Services is inaccurate, you have the right to dispute it. Common reasons include:
• Wrong balance amount
• Account is not mine (identity theft)
• Already paid or settled
• Incorrect closure reason
• Other data inaccuracy
How to file a dispute
To dispute your EWS data, mail a written letter to the address below. Include your full name, account number, the specific data you are disputing, and any supporting documentation.
✉
Mail your dispute to
SoFi Bank, N.A.
2750 East Cottonwood Parkway #300

What to include in your letter
✓ Your full legal name
✓ Your SoFi account number or reference number
✓ The specific data you believe is inaccurate
✓ A brief explanation of why it is incorrect
✓ Any supporting documents (copies, not originals)
Your rights under FCRA: SoFi will investigate your dispute within 30 days and notify you of the outcome. You have the right to add a personal statement to your record.
